Project Overview

I had been asked as part of a Consulting Group to Analyze the Dataset and peform Machine Learning models in order to see how certain social environmental factors affect the life expectancy in different counties in the USA

Findings After analyzing and cleaning we can see the main contributers to Years of Potential Life lost Rate (sum of difference between age of death and average age of death, in other words this would be the total of all the peoples difference between their age and the average age of death in the USA which is related to life expectancy, is expected age of death higher or lower than the average in the USA) for each county was most affected by these features:

  • %_LBW - Percentage of births with low birth weight (under 2500g)
  • %_Obese - Percentage of adults that report BMI >= 30
  • Food Environment index - Indicator of access to healthy foods

Key Features

Data Collection & Cleaning

Collected and cleaned county-level social, environmental, and health data for the United States to ensure it was ready for analysis.

Data Exploration & Visualization

Explored the datasets using descriptive statistics and visualizations to uncover patterns, trends, and correlations between social/environmental factors and life expectancy.

Modeling & Analysis

Applied statistical and machine learning models to measure the impact of different factors on life expectancy, identifying the most influential variables.

Communication of Insights

Documented the results with clear visualizations and summaries, making the findings easy to understand and highlighting actionable insights about county-level life expectancy differences.
